Bring Mosquito Education to Life!

Free, Interactive STEM Workshops for SF Youth

The Healthy Housing and Vector Control Program is proud to offer hands-on educational workshops. We bring the science lab directly to you, turning local environmental health into an engaging, real-world learning experience!

Where We Go

Our program is 100% FREE and available to any youth-serving organization located within the City and County of San Francisco, including:

  • Schools (Public, Private, & Charter)
  • Summer Camps & Day Camps
  • Community & After-School Programs
  • Science Centers & Museums
  • Scout Troops & Youth Clubs

Tailored for All Ages

We adapt our curriculum to be age-appropriate, highly accessible, and aligned with student learning capacities:

Elementary (K–5)

Focus on basic biology, the life cycle, and interactive bug observation.

Middle School (6–8)

Deeper dive into habitats, ecosystems, and introductory public health.

High School (9–12)

Advanced focus on vector-borne diseases, IPM, and environmental science careers.

Sample Content & Activities

We set up a dynamic, multi-station mobile lab tailored to your schedule (typically 45–60 minutes). Activities include:

1

Microscope Exploration

Students use real lab microscopes to observe the intricate anatomy of mosquito larvae, pupae, and adults.

2

Metamorphosis Deep Dive

A hands-on look at the four stages of the mosquito life cycle and how they adapt to water environments.

3

Public Health Detective

Learning how mosquitoes transmit diseases and how to protect our families and pets effectively.

4

Environmental Stewardship

An interactive activity identifying "standing water" hazards and learning how to prevent neighborhood infestations.
